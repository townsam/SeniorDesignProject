## **Project Focus**

Unity-based puzzle game in which actors, each with randomized movement traits like jump strength, speed, and direction, must solve puzzles by interacting with the environment. Players will influence the actors’ behavior through different tools that affect the reward function on actors such as placing flags which reward actors that get closer or further away. These tools serve as goals or checkpoints, guiding the actors toward solving the puzzle.

The actors are driven by reinforcement learning, where they receive rewards based on things such as their proximity to flags or other puzzle objectives. Over time, the actors learn to navigate the environment and adjust their actions to efficiently reach the flags and complete the puzzle.
